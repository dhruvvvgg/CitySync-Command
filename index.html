The Problem

The Anatomy of a Gridlock

Modern cities operate on legacy traffic management protocols. When an anomaly occurs, such as a heavy vehicle breakdown, localized waterlogging, or a sudden protest, control rooms rely on static cameras and human intuition.

The result is a delayed operational response that allows a localized incident to trigger a city-wide arterial cascade within minutes. 94% of these severe congestion events are entirely unplanned.

Trust & Bounding

Failsafe Predictions

A system that is wrong twice is never used again. CitySync quantifies its own prediction uncertainties.

Instead of predicting a single timeline, we calculate confidence bounds. Operators see best-case, worst-case, and expected outcomes, ensuring complete operational safety.

Continuous Learning

Every Incident Makes Us Smarter

CitySync doesn't just evaluate traffic; it evaluates its own recommendations. By analyzing operator adjustments, officer arrival times, and road recovery curves, the platform refines its prediction weightings.

The system acts as a digital twin, getting progressively more accurate for your city's specific topology every day.
